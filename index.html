~7–8 km · ~30 min riding

🟢 Route 51 South Coast Half-Loop
~20–22 km · ~1.5–2 hrs riding
Turns south onto Rt. 51 after the bridge, high above the coast with citrus orchards & sea views. Features the famous "Yahoo! Hill" (nickname only — not on Google Maps) — also called "Lightning Bolt Hill", an S-curve descent near the Toyo Ito Museum of Architecture that's so fun riders scream "Yahoo!" going down. Intermediate level with long climbs.
